Plot

In Criminal Minds, the show follows a team of FBI Agents. These agents are from the BAU, or behavioral analysis unit. What this means is that they use their knowledge of psychology and criminal to 'get into the criminals minds' to catch them and bring them to justice.

Throughout the show, many different cenarios occur. If you want to learn about some of these, go to the Spoilers Tab. In every episode, there is a different criminal that has a different motive, style of killing, and result. Ths keeps the show constantl;y intersenting.

At the same time, the lives of the Agents are shown as well. We get to see different events in their life and how their work effects them and how the events effect their work.
